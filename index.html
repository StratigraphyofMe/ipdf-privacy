Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

1. Information Collection

iPDF app does not collect any personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.) from its users. All PDF files and photos you process are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external servers.

2. File Access

File access permissions (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) required for our app to work are used only for you to select, edit, and save PDF files. These permissions are necessary for the core functionality of the app and are not used for any other purpose.

3. Third Party Services

Our application can work completely offline. It does not send data to any server, does not use analytics services, and does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

4. Security

The security of your data is important to us. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. It is your responsibility to ensure the security of your device and the files you process.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.
